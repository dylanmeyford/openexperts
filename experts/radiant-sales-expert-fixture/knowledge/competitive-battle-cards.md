# Competitive Battle Cards

Competitive intelligence for common competitors encountered in deals.

## Competitor A — LegacyCRM Pro

- **Positioning**: Enterprise-first, heavy customization.
- **Weakness**: Slow implementation (6-12 months), high total cost of ownership.
- **Counter**: Emphasize time-to-value and modern UX. Reference customer migrations.

## Competitor B — QuickSell Cloud

- **Positioning**: SMB-focused, low price point.
- **Weakness**: Limited reporting, no multi-currency support, poor API.
- **Counter**: Highlight scalability gaps. Show ROI at enterprise volumes.

## Competitor C — Status Quo (Do Nothing)

- **Positioning**: Buyer stalls or deprioritizes the project.
- **Weakness**: Ongoing cost of inaction (manual processes, missed revenue).
- **Counter**: Quantify cost of delay using buyer's own metrics.
